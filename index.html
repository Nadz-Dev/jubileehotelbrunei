About Us

A Landmark of Brunei Hospitality

Named in honour of a royal jubilee, the Jubilee Hotel has welcomed travellers to Bandar Seri Begawan for decades. Our prime city-centre location puts you steps from Brunei's most treasured landmarks, while our dedicated staff ensure every guest feels at home.

We offer spacious rooms at exceptional value, complimentary airport transfers, late checkout options, and an on-site cafe serving delicious local cuisine. Whether you're a solo explorer, business traveller, or family on holiday — Jubilee Hotel is your base camp for discovering Brunei.

Cancellation Policy
Free cancellation up to 48 hours before check-in. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a one-night charge. No-shows will be charged the full reservation amount.

Children & Extra Beds
Children under 5 stay free in existing beds. Extra rollaway beds available at BND 20/night. Maximum occupancy applies per room type. Cribs available on request.
🐶
Pets Policy
Unfortunately, pets are not permitted in the hotel. Assistance animals are welcome with prior notification and documentation. Please contact us to arrange.
🚪
Smoking Policy
All rooms and indoor areas are strictly non-smoking. Designated outdoor smoking areas are available. A cleaning fee of BND 100 applies for violations.
💳
Payment & Deposit
A refundable security deposit of BND 20 is collected at check-in. We accept cash (BND), major credit/debit cards. Online bookings require a valid card at time of reservation.
🧭
Alcohol Policy
Brunei is an alcohol-free country. The sale and public consumption of alcohol is prohibited. Guests must respect all local laws and hotel guidelines during their stay.
🏭
Dress Code & Respect
We request that guests dress modestly in public areas. When visiting nearby mosques, modest attire covering shoulders and knees is required. Respectful conduct always.
Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I arrange the complimentary airport transfer? ⌄
Email us at least 24 hours before your arrival with your flight number, arrival time, and number of guests. Our driver will meet you at the arrivals hall holding a Jubilee Hotel sign. The service runs 7am–9pm daily. For late-night arrivals (9pm–6am), a surcharge of BND 20 applies.
Is breakfast included in the room rate? ⌄
Yes! All room rates include a complimentary breakfast voucher worth BND 3 per person, redeemable at Sunway Kopitiam on the ground floor. You can choose from several set breakfast options (e.g. chicken rice). Upgrades are available from the full menu at additional cost.
Is free parking available at the hotel? ⌄
Yes, ample free parking is available on-site for all registered hotel guests. The parking area is accessible 24 hours. Please note that the parking is for hotel guests only — our security team ensures fair use.
Can I get a late check-out? ⌄
Late check-out until 1:00 PM is available at no extra charge, subject to availability. Please inform the front desk the evening before or in the morning of your departure. We do our best to accommodate all requests.
How far is the hotel from major attractions? ⌄
The Omar Ali Saifuddien Mosque is approximately 750–900 m away (10-min walk). Tamu Kianggeh market is just 100 m away. Kampung Ayer water village boat jetties are 300 m. The Royal Regalia Museum is an 8-min walk. Most of BSB city centre is accessible on foot.
Is Wi-Fi available in all rooms? ⌄
Yes, complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel including all guest rooms. Signal strength may vary by floor and room location. If you experience connectivity issues, please contact our front desk and we will do our best to resolve them promptly.
What currency is used and are cards accepted? ⌄
Brunei Darussalam Dollar (BND) is the official currency, which is interchangeable 1:1 with Singapore Dollar (SGD). We accept major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard) as well as cash. A card terminal is available at the front desk.
Is the hotel suitable for families with young children? ⌄
Absolutely! Our Family Suite offers over 60 m² with 2 bedrooms and a living room — ideal for families. Children under 5 stay free in existing beds. Cribs and rollaway beds are available on request. The hotel is also within easy walking distance of family-friendly attractions.
